Our Story

Authentic Indian Cuisine Since 1995

Sweet India Family Restaurant brings you the true essence of Indian culinary traditions. Founded by Chef Rajesh Kumar in 1995, we've been serving authentic flavors with a touch of home-style cooking.

Our recipes come from generations of family traditions, using only the freshest ingredients and aromatic spices imported directly from India.
